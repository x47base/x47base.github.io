Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 4, 2025

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Account data (email/username) and trade entries (symbols, prices, profit/loss, timestamps).
  • Automatically Collected: Device info (model, OS), usage data (screens viewed, features used), and crash reports.
  • Third-Party Integrations: Push tokens for notifications, optional analytics data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to run and improve the App, store trades locally, send reminders, and analyze usage. No trade data is stored on our servers.

3. Information Sharing

  • We do not sell or trade your personal data.
  • We share only with trusted service providers (e.g., notification services) on your phone.
  • We do not store any of your data on our servers.

4. Security & Storage

All trade data is stored locally on your device via AsyncStorage. We use industry-standard security measures, but no system is 100% secure.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App isn’t intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

6. Your Choices

  • Disable notifications via your device settings.
  • Uninstall the App to clear local data.

7. Updates

We may update this policy; changes will be posted here with a new “Last updated” date.
